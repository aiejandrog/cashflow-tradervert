# Monetization & Audience Funnel — Tradervert Games

The model: **free games build the audience; paid games make the money.** Freedom Race is the free funnel. Every update keeps players engaged and converts them into *followers*, who then get notified of — and buy — future premium titles.

## The funnel
```
Players ──(in-game "Follow" nudges)──▶ itch followers
   │                                        │
   │  free updates (devlogs)                │  notified of every new release
   ▼                                        ▼
 Tips/donations (now)            Buy PAID future games (later)
```

## Phase 1 — NOW: grow the audience (keep it free)
- **Pricing:** Freedom Race stays **free with "name your own price" / donations** (itch → game → Edit → Pricing → "$0 or donate", set a suggested tip like $3). Free = maximum reach.
- **In-game hooks (already built):**
  - Win screen → **❤️ Follow for new games** + **☕ Support the dev** buttons (peak-satisfaction ask).
  - Version badge → "What's New" changelog → **❤️ Follow** button.
- **Goal:** maximize followers. Followers are the asset — they're who you sell to later.

## Phase 2 — LATER: monetize with premium games
- Ship new, polished titles as **paid games** ($2.99–$7.99) under a **Tradervert Games** itch presence.
- Cross-promote to the follower base the free game built: each new title gets a **devlog** (auto-notifies followers) + a "More games by Tradervert" cross-link added to Freedom Race.
- Optionally bundle multiple games for a discount as the catalog grows.

## Why this works
- itch **devlogs notify every follower for free** — no email infra, no ad spend.
- A free, genuinely good game is the cheapest possible top-of-funnel.
- You only ask for money (tips now, purchases later) at high-intent moments (a win, a new release) — never nag screens.

## Levers to revisit later (only if the audience is there)
- Patreon/membership for early access + Discord role (recurring income; needs steady output).
- A paid "supporter"/cosmetic unlock inside a free game.
- Keep these OFF until follower count justifies the effort.
